# Farmer beta landing copy

## Primary headline

See what nearby grain buyers are posting before you decide where to sell.

## Supporting copy

GrainBids brings local corn, soybean, and wheat bids into one market view so you can compare locations, delivery periods, and market movement without checking every buyer's website yourself.

Eastern Ontario is the starting market. The beta is free while we learn which buyers, locations, alerts, and historical views are genuinely useful to farmers across Ontario.

## Primary CTA

Join the free farmer beta

## CTA helper text

Get access to the current market view and help decide what GrainBids builds next. No trading advice. No commitment. Unsubscribe anytime.

## What users can do now

- compare posted bids by commodity, location, and delivery period;
- see the source and timing behind a market observation where available;
- identify where a bid or delivery period is missing or stale;
- tell GrainBids which nearby buyers and views matter most.

## What is coming based on validated feedback

- nearby-buyer comparison;
- meaningful bid-change alerts;
- historical basis and price charts;
- clearer delivery-period and crop-year filters.

## Trust note

Posted bids are market indications, not guaranteed executable offers. GrainBids shows the source and context so farmers can verify the quote directly with the buyer.

## Do not claim

Do not claim that GrainBids finds the best price everywhere, improves sale results, covers all Ontario buyers, predicts markets, or replaces a farmer's own marketing decision.
